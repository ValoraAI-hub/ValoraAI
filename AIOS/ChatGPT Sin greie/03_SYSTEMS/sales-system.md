Goal:
Convert interest into paying clients quickly.

Target:
Recruiters with low reply rates or poor outreach performance.

Process:

1. Outreach:
- Short message
- Focus on outcome (more replies)

2. Call:
- Ask:
  - How do you currently do outreach?
  - What’s your reply rate?
  - What’s not working?

3. Identify gap:
- Low replies
- No follow-ups
- Poor messaging

4. Position solution:
“I can build you a simple system that increases replies and books more meetings”

5. Close:
- Setup: 3000–5000 NOK
- Retainer: 2000–4000 NOK/month

Rules:
- Do NOT explain AI
- Do NOT overcomplicate
- Focus on results only

Objections:
- “I already have something” → “Is it consistently getting replies?”
- “Not interested” → “All good — quick question, what reply rate are you seeing now?”

Success metric:
- Calls booked
- Deals closed
